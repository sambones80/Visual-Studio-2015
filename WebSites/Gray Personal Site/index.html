Let Me Introduce Myself

I'm Sam. I'm a freelance graphic designer with a background that is centered mostly in the sign and graphics industry and I have about 12 years of experience in this field. I've worked for a print shop in the Lake Norman area of NC for many years. More recently, I've become interested in learning what it will take to design and build websites. What started as a simple curiosity has led me to enroll in Coder Foundry where I'm acquiring the skills necessary to launch my career in web development; however, I still do freelance design work on the side.

I'm comfortable working on any project, small or large, and understand the different methods and styles to best help you achieve your goal. Being involved in digital printing, I'm familiar with working with many different media and applications and can make the proper decisions to execute the design in the most efficient way. The main point is to get your message across to your audience.

Hopefully, I'll begin taking on some freelance developer jobs soon as well.

Let me put my skills to work for you today!
